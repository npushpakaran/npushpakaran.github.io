My Blog

As a computer science graduate student, I spend a lot of time learning—through lectures, projects, trial and errors, and late night bug fixes.

Some lessons are quick wins. Others take days. I realized that writing about these will not only help me retain—but might also help someone else who’s on a similar path!

This blog is a space to:

  • Share what I’m learning
  • Break down complex topics
  • Reflect on my journey—challenges, wins and growth
  • Document projects, ideas and random code experiences

I don’t claim to be an expert, but I believe in sharing —small stuff adds value. Thanks for reading.
